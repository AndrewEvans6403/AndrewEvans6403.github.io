Pathfinding
GUI

A graphical user interface created in pygame to visually demonstrate Djikstra's and Breadth First Search algorithmns.

This GUI was created in the first 2 weeks of summer vacation my 2nd year of undergrad. This was right after I took a data structures course and wanted to combine my love for graphics and the new knowledge I learned of pathfinding.

The green tiles signify what nodes have been considered for the given shortest path. You can left click to add/remove walls and change the grid. Right click moves the destination node and middle click moves the start node. Click the github link below to go to the project repo.
